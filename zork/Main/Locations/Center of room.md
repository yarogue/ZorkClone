#location 
#gameplay 
This is middle or Center [[Cell]] part of room where [[Chair]] is stays and player start game for first time . On [[Look]] command player can observe 6 directions and on [[Go]] command player can move all of them except up and down . In addition player can command to [[Smell]] on which player will have such [[Commentary]] : "That was a mistake...". ;
	Look up:
		On Looking up in this location player will see ceiling of room and have such [[Commentary]]:
		"A bare fluorescent light flickers.The ceilings is pretty high in here...".
	Look down:
		On Looking down in this location player will see ceramic dirty floor have such [[Commentary]]:
		"Cold concrete floor with scattered dust.This place is dirty..."
	Look North:
		On Looking north in this location player will see [[Front center]] of [[Room]]. Player Can [[Command]] to [[Go]] or [[Look]] at that direction. When player look at this direction they will see [[Exit door]]. Player will have such [[Commentary]] : 
		"This direction is most probably where i need to go ...".
	Look South:
		On Looking north in this location player will see [[Back center]] of [[Room]].Player Can [[Command]] to [[Go]] or [[Look]] at that direction.When player look at this direction they will see [[Back door]]. Player will have such [[Commentary]] :
		"This is opposite of where i need to go".
